  • Your full name

If you wish to have personal information removed from our testing phase, create an Issue on the GitHub repository, or email one of the developers.
The Application does not utilise any sort of cookie or device-local storage, for any reason. We do not observe, collect, retain, or share with a third party, any data which is not explicitly granted to us when you log in with Google.
The Application does not collect data about your device specifications, user agent, browsing history, other cookies, advertising IDs, or other information not listed above.
The Application stores non-identifying data about your use history, and current user state. This data includes, but is not limited to:

  • The time you last used the Application
